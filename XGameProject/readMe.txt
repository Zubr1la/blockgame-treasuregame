Some information about this gamemode.

1. You need atleast 5 bricks to start game.

2. To place bricks, press on the corresponding button, and click on game screen. You can not add bricks too close to the paddle.

3. To remove bricks, press on the corresponding button and click on existing brick. It should disappear. 

4. To save your created level, simply enter what filename you want and press "Save bricks". 

5. To load just saved level, restart game and it should be available in level dropdown.

6. You can't place bricks on top of each other, and some other information can be seen in console log, if it seems like nothing is happening.
